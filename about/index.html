about

Hi, I'm Trevor!

I'm a father, husband, D&D 5e DM, and Oxford comma enthusiast.

I started this blog to try and take ownership of my digital identity – ever since the Twitter meltdown it's felt like the web is going back to a more decentralized model.

This site is built using Publii, a static site generator that is a little less intense than something like Jekyll or Hugo.

I grew up and still live in New England with my wife, son, daughter, three cats, and two fish. I work for a Fortune 500 company as a director of cloud engineering.

I enjoy: fintech, science (both real and fiction), coffee, music, gadgets and tech, self-hosting.
